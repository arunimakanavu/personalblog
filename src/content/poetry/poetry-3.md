An ocean, I am  
Of depths, of trenches  
Of mysteries  
Of existence, of beats  
Of poetry  
Of darkness.

I would embrace you  
With tiny waves  
In a hot afternoon.  
Would salt your lips  
Like mischievous breeze.

You can fall in love  
With my calmness.  
It's waves will  
Fall in love with you too.  
They'll take you to the shore  
Before the tempest  
Before the surge  
Before the calamity.

Walk away.  
Look back never.  
Perceive the darkness neither.  
For the depth that knows you  
The colours of it  
Won't let you escape.
