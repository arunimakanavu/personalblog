The earth was barely asleep  
Like a mother with a crying child  
Worn out, but stayed awake for.  
Sagittarius was conspicuously visible  
Boasting it's presence in the sky.

And the Antares  
After years, was speaking to me  
Or I was listening to her  
Of all the times she has shined  
Of all the times she has burnt  
Of all the nights she watched me for  
Of all the years I've never looked upto

Yet she wasn't complaining  
She wasn't drowned in despair  
She shredded her tears of dew  
It ached all the way through my heart.

I've been through these woods very often  
But tonight seemed denser.  
A mere ray of light didn't penetrate through  
Not a little silence made a noise  
Echo was blindfolded with the mischievous fog.

Amidst all the disheartening,  
She was singing  
Dulcetly written and composed  
Had a lifetime of joy  
And a deathtime of sorrows,  
Forever untold,  
Travelled light years.

Ah, Finally tonight  
I wasn't pretending,  
I wasn't ranting,  
Uttering a not word,  
I was listening.
