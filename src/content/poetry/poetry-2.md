An Ode to the Year  

The vicissitudes of shadows  
That followed around  
Darkened the words.  
Ephemerality of conversations  
Flickered the pavement.  

The musings  
Yet to be smoldered off  
Shaded the days.  

The nights passed by  
With myriads of delusions,  
Beliefs of none.  

Travelled far   
From where it was caved in.  
Dived deeper  
From where it was drowned in.  

Embellished with the soul slivers  
The fetters clanked  
Farther-afar.  
Cacophony of obsessions,  
Muzzled obligations,  
Agonising compassions,  
Oxymorons of apprehensions,  
Cantillated,  
Farther afar to tranquility  
Farther afar from illusions.
